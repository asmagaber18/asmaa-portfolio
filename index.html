Career Path

My Professional Journey

"

My career journey started in manufacturing, where I gained hands-on experience in factory hiring while working with LG. This foundation gave me strong exposure to operational hiring and the dynamics of blue-collar recruitment.

I then joined Haier at a critical stage during the construction phase, where I played a key role in building the workforce from the ground up. Starting with a core team of around 30 employees, I contributed to scaling operations and supporting the hiring for 4 factories, reaching a workforce of approximately 1,200 employees.

This experience strengthened my ability to manage large-scale hiring, operate under pressure, and align recruitment efforts with business expansion and operational needs.

Later, I joined Etisal during a transformative phase, as the company entered a strategic partnership with Honor. This role exposed me to a more complex and dynamic environment, where I handled a diverse mix of hiring needs across:

  • Manufacturing & operations
  • Sales & marketing
  • Commercial functions

Balancing these different hiring streams allowed me to develop a broader business perspective, combining operational hiring with corporate and commercial talent acquisition.

Overall, my journey has shaped me into a Talent Acquisition professional who can adapt to different environments, manage scale, and contribute to building teams that support real business growth.

This journey enabled me to move beyond traditional recruitment into a more strategic role, where hiring directly supports business performance and expansion.
